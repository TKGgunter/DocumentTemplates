# Descriptive title

## Glossary

**Term**: Short paragraph describing the issue.

## Introduction / Problem

Describe the problem:
- why it's a problem
- who cares
- why they care
- what happens if we do nothing
- what we gain if we solve the problem


## Solutions

Include any high level description of the solutions. Include caveats and
general points of emphasis.

### Possible Solutions

Give a high level description of the specific solution. 

**Pros:**
- list internal, and external benefits of the solution. Include costs, time
  spent, number of teams, ownership simplicity/complexity of the solution.

**Cons:**
- list internal, and external drawbacks.

## Recommendations

Recommend one of the solution listed in possible solutions section.
