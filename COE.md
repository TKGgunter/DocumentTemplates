# Summary
Introduce the problem service - what the service is used for and how it impacts
customers. Who owns the service. Introduce the offending incident. When did it
occur. Why did it occur. How did the incident impact customers.

Use data as qualifiers in place of descriptive language.

# Metrics and Graphs
Identify key graphs that paint a picture of the issue and label the graph so
that it is clear to the reader when the event occurred and the resulting
impact.
