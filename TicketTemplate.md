# Description(Problem)

> Describe the problem, the customer impact and why and the degree of its
> importance. Additionally, provide relevant context. If it is a bug provide the
> steps to replicate it. It never hurts to provide more detail.
> 
> **Remember:** You may not be the one working on this story.
> Your teammates may not have you level of context. The level of detail you
> provide will influence how quickly they will be able to get up to speed.
